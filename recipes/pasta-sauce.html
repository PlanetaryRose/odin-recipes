Homemade Pasta Sauce

A homemade pasta sauce not only means you can customize it to suit your tastes, but it also brings any pasta dish to the next level. I keep my cooking simple and intuitive. I keep mine simple for an easy, quick and delicious dinner.

You can't beat a homemade pasta sauce, with a base of tomato and meat that can be customized with whatever ingredients you want!

Ingredients

  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • half an onion, diced
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon of sugar
  • oregano, salt, pepper, thyme, chilli flakes, and any other spices you want
  • Cornstarch, for thickening the sauce if desired

Cooking Steps

  1. In a large saucepan on high heat, add the ground beef, breaking it into chunks and cooking thoroughly.
  2. Next, add the diced onion and cook for at least a minute or two.
  3. Next, add the minced garlic followed by the can of diced tomatoes. Include the water from the can. If needed for a larger batch, add half a can of additional water.
  4. Bring the mixture to just about a simmer. Taste the sauce and if you find the taste a bit too acidic, then add the sugar and stir. Taste again and add more sugar if needed.
  5. Lower the heat as needed to keep the sauce at a simmer and stir occasionally. After about 3-5 mins of cooking, add spices to taste. Turn off the heat but leave the sauce on the burner,
  6. Finally, if you want a thicker sauce, take about 1 teaspoon upto a tablespoon of cornstarch in a glass, and add a similar amount of cold water. Stir until combined and then add this to the sauce and stir until thickened.
  7. If you make the sauce too thick, simply add more water or tomato sauce, and return the pan to the heat as needed.
